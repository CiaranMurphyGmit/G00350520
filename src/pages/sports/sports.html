Sports Venues

Here is a list of sports venues in Galway.

Pearse Stadium

Pearse Stadium is the principal GAA stadium in County Galway, Ireland. The Galway GAA Gaelic football and hurling teams use the stadium for their home games.

Rating: 3.9

Address: Salthill, Galway

Capacity: 26,197

Opened: June 16, 1957

Galway Sportsgrounds

Galway Sportsground, also known as The Sportsground and the Galway Greyhound Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Galway, Ireland.

Rating: 4.5

Address: 23 Coole Park, Bohermore, Galway, H91 E2HC

Capacity: 8,100

Opened: 1927

Eamonn Deacy Park

Eamonn Deacy Park, formerly known as Terryland Park, is an association football stadium in the Republic of Ireland based in the Terryland district of Galway.

Rating: 4.4

Address: Dyke Rd, Galway

Capacity: 5,000 (3,300 seated)

Team: Galway United F.C.

Drom Soccer Park

Drom Soccer Park is an association football venue in the Republic of Ireland based in Drom East, Rahoon, County Galway. It is the home ground of Salthill Devon F.C..

Rating: 4.6

Address: Drum East, Tonabrocky, Drum East, Co. Galway

Capacity: 2,000

Opened: 2002
